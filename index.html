Tips to Spot Suspicious Emails

Check the Sender

Make sure the email address is trustworthy. Small differences can indicate phishing.

Don't Click Suspicious Links

Hover over links to see their actual destination before clicking.

Be Careful with Attachments

Avoid opening unexpected attachments, especially .exe, .zip, or suspicious documents.

Watch for Grammar Errors

Typos and strange formatting are common warning signs.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Even if a malicious email steals your password, 2FA adds an extra layer of protection.
